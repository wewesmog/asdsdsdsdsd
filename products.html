  • Flexible Amounts: Borrow from KES 1,000 to KES 50,000, tailored to your specific needs
  • Swift Processing: Get funds in your account within 15 minutes of approval
  • Competitive Rates: Enjoy our market-leading interest rate of just 0.1% per day
  • No Hidden Fees: Transparent pricing with no surprise charges

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Age: 21-55 years old
  • Income: Regular source of income (employed or self-employed)
  • Documentation: Valid Kenyan ID and proof of income
  • Credit Score: No minimum credit score required for first-time borrowers

Repayment Options

  • Flexible Terms: Choose from 7, 14, 21, or 30 days
  • Multiple Channels: Repay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or in-app payment
  • Early Repayment: No penalties for early settlement
  • Auto-Debit: Set up automatic payments to avoid late fees
Frequently Asked Questions
How quickly can I get the loan?

Once approved, funds are disbursed within 15 minutes to your registered mobile money or bank account.

What documents do I need?

Valid Kenyan ID, proof of income (3 months bank statements or payslips), and utility bill for address verification.

How is the interest calculated?

Interest is charged at 0.1% per day on the outstanding loan amount. For example, a KES 10,000 loan for 30 days would accrue KES 300 in interest.

Can I apply for multiple loans?

You must fully repay your current loan before applying for a new one. Good repayment history may qualify you for higher amounts.

What happens if I can't repay on time?

Contact us immediately to discuss extension options. Late payments incur additional fees and may affect your credit score.

Is there a credit check?

We perform a soft credit check using alternative data sources including mobile money history and social media activity.

Can I repay early?

Yes, early repayment is encouraged and comes with no penalties. This can improve your credit score.

What if my application is rejected?

You'll receive detailed feedback and can reapply after 30 days. We'll provide tips to improve your chances.

How do I check my loan status?

Track your loan status real-time through our app or by logging into your account on our website.

What are the repayment methods?

Repay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or set up automatic deductions from your salary.

Can I increase my loan limit?

Yes, consistent timely repayments can qualify you for higher limits up to KES 50,000.

What if there's an emergency?

Our 24/7 customer support can help restructure your loan in case of genuine emergencies.
